Organizations don't change. People do.

That's the fundamental truth that most change management frameworks miss. You can redesign processes, restructure teams, and roll out new technology — but if the people aren't ready, willing, and able to change, nothing moves.

## Why Change Fails

The most common reasons organizational change fails:

- **No compelling "why"** — people need to understand the reason
- **Leadership inconsistency** — saying one thing, rewarding another
- **Fear unaddressed** — change threatens identity and security
- **One-size-fits-all approach** — different people need different support

## A Better Approach

Effective change starts with understanding the human experience of transition. It requires patience, empathy, and a willingness to listen before acting.
