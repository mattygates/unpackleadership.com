Working across cultures isn't just about speaking different languages. It's about understanding different ways of thinking, different expectations around hierarchy, and different approaches to conflict and feedback.

## The Invisible Barriers

Some of the biggest communication challenges are invisible. They're not about what's said — they're about what's assumed.

- **Direct vs. indirect communication styles**
- **Individual vs. collective decision-making**
- **Different relationships with time and deadlines**
- **Varying comfort with hierarchy and authority**

## Why It Matters Now

Remote and hybrid work has made intercultural communication more important than ever. When your team spans continents, cultural fluency becomes a daily necessity — not a nice-to-have.

## What's Ahead

This series will explore practical strategies for communicating effectively across cultural boundaries. From navigating different feedback styles to building trust across time zones.
